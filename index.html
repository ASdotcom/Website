Flexible Circuit Test Fixture

Fixture for contacting sheets of small flexible circuits for RF testing.

Flexible Circuit Test Fixture
Top Down View

Here you can see the test fixture in the closed and latched postion, ready for testing.

Open View

Here you can see the contact pins, bearings, and air springs that hold the fixture open. We took advantage of the transparent material and used lasers to align the sheets.

Previous Next

I designed this test fixture as a part of consulting job with Odin Engineering. I did the full design and passed it off to be manufactured as I was starting work full time. The fixture holds down a sheet of small flexible antennas and makes contact in 144 points without damaging the circuits. It then communicates with the circuits during RF testing, thus the requirement to minimize metal in the design.
